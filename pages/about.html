Nicholas Silva - About Me

Hi!

I'm a senior English major at the University of Massachusetts, Amherst! I'm currently pursuing certificates in creative writing and Professional Writing and Technical Communication.

I think that it's quite fair, and sums me up quite well, to say that I am a massive nerd. I have a longstanding passion for reading and writing, and love all things science fiction, fantasy, and musical.

I have been writing creatively since the fourth grade, and am also very fascinated by the technical side of writing. I love to book bind, and have taught myself how to format texts using Word and Adobe InDesign!

I also love video games, and hope to write for them one day! In my free time, I have been known to dabble with pixel art and 3D modeling, and even a spot of game development. Who knows, by the time you're reading this there might even be a game or two on this portfolio!

When I'm not writing, reading, or noodling around with tech you might find me hiking or spending time with friends gushing over movies or singing along to the latest Broadway hit.

Some “Fun Facts” About Me:

  • My favorite book is JRR Tolkien's The Hobbit
  • Some of my favorite media franchises include: Star Wars, Star Trek, and Lord of the Rings
  • Some of my favorite video games include Minecraft, Sims 4, and Jedi: Fallen Order
