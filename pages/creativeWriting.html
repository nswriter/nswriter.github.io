Above are two short stories that I wrote during a creative writing workshop focused on flash fiction during the fall 2024 semester. They reflect my musings on the changing seasons, and a preparation for the "spooky season". Something about the limitations of flash fiction really spoke to me, and filled me with inspiration; I really enjoyed this exercise.

I have always enjoyed creative writing, and am always churning through ideas for the next story in my mind. A walk around campus, an overheard conversation on campus, exploring, and especially nature; my senses are always open to possibilities.
