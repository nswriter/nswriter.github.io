My Articles for the Massachusetts Daily Collegian

2022

At the start of Sophmore year I wrote a few articles for UMass' newspaper, the Daily Collegian as a correspondent.

Visit

Oh academic writing. I can't say that writing essays is my passion, but there is something to their authenticity and elegance that I must admit is comforting. And, not to brag too much, but I have always prided myself on my essay writing skills -especially my ability to quickly turn one out at a good quality.

Above is an essay from a class I took on later British literature. I do not allege that it is the best essay I have ever written, but there is something about it that I quite liked. I love nature very much, and so I guess I can't resist the draw of an essay all about the relationship between its depiction in two works with such rich natural imagery.

I have also included a link to the three articles that I wrote for the Massachusetts Daily Collegian during October 2022. I very much enjoyed writing for the paper, and do somewhat regret not continuing. Alas, time alluded me and here we are, but they are a fond and welcome memory in my writing career, and an homage to the school journalism that helped shape me into the writer, and the person, that I am today.
