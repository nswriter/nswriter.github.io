My Writing Journey

I began to write creatively in fourth grade, penning a series of stories about dragons on small, yellow legal pages between lessons. From then on, I knew that I wanted to write and be around writing no matter where my journey took me.

I like to think that I go above and beyond when it comes to writing. Any chance I am offered for creative writing I endeavor to turn into an epic project. Over the course of middle school to high school I turned two creative writing projects into novellas, a weekly vocabulary exercise into a serialized story, and even took an independent study to churn out a novel-length project!

At the same time, my professional writing journey was quietly beginning. In ninth grade I was recruited to my high school's student newspaper, the Paw Print. I thoroughly enjoyed my time in the paper, and all of the challenges that it posed. Soon, I was promoted to an editor position, and later editor-in-chief! During this time, I was tasked with overseeing our transition to an online format.

When I turned my attention to university and my future, I knew that a degree in English was the only logical pursuit. I had my eyes set on a future in publishing and creative writing, and joined the creative writing certificate program instantly. As time progressed, however, I decided to diversify to broaden my horizons and itch my technical interests, thus begining my journey on the Professional Writing and Technical Communication certificate program. This was certainly a great choice! I have very much enjoyed my time learning all about documentation, writing for the web (and web design itself!) and more.

I can't wait to see where my writing career takes me next!
